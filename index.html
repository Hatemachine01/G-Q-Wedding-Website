About the bride

Originally from California but raised in Arizona, Gaby took a cross country trip to find a new place to call home, she settled in Oregon in August of 2017 where she would unknowingly find the love of her life in 2019. For the past six years, she has dedicated herself to helping animals as a certified veterinary assistant, not just in the states but also in Peru. Along with being a certified vet assistant, she has certifications in yoga and bartending, and plans on getting a couple more certifications or degrees in the near future. Gaby’s favorite things to do are garden, watch movies, nap, travel, read, hike, and eat sushi with Quentin.

About the groom

Oregon born and raised, Quentin moved to New York City in the fall of 2015 for a graduate program in Psychology, and found himself looking for a much needed dose of adventure alongside. Wanting to move back closer to family (and towards slightly cheaper rent), Quentin relocated to his home state with an employment opportunity in a rural coastal locale. Soon bored and wanting more, Quentin found exactly what he didn’t know he needed: his best friend and the love of his life, Gabriela Plascencia.

Our story

March 3rd, 2019

First Date

Gaby and Quentin’s first date was at the Horse Brass Pub in Portland; consisting of beer, cider, potato wedges, and the cheesiest decor Portland could offer. Although Quentin spoke nearly the entire time (which is still the norm), Gaby fell for his beautiful smile and sweet demeanor. Quentin fell for Gaby at first sight, and their love continued to blossom from there.

July 11th ,2019

The Move In

The two decided to move in with one another following Gaby’s search for a new home. They decided to settle in a small third floor apartment in Beaverton. While showing her mother around the new place via Facetime, Gaby accidentally slipped the news of her new living arrangement and ‘roommate.’ Gaby’s mother exclaimed, “You keep surprising me.”

October 3rd, 2019; July 23rd, 2020

Growing Family

The young couple decided to grow their family with a set of four paws. Penelope was a rescue hunting dog from the Humane Society. Broken, friendly, and needing a forever home, the two took her in with open arms as their first companion. Hearing of their success with dogs of the hunting family, the young couple took another four legged companion in less than a year later from a friend of the family. Sweet, goofy, and terribly dumb, Eevee completed the family.

June 19th, 2020

Proposal and Engagement

Quentin knew he found the person who he wanted to spend the rest of his life with, and that there was one additional step of which he needed to follow through on to make this happen. Ring in hand and poor at keeping secrets from his now fiancee, Quentin took Gaby to a small coastal community of Cannon Beach where the two had first kissed. Three cities, two dogs, and one awful pandemic later, they’re celebrating their love in the best possible way, and are ecstatic that everyone’s in attendance.
